About me


My name is Cheddi Charles. I am the oldest of five brothers. I was born from the beautiful island of Grenada located south of the Caribbean sea. Growing up was not easy because we have to work hard to get what you want.

Sports is one of my favorite hobbies. I play cricket, soccer and basketball whenever I get the chance. I always dream of playing for my country but I was not good enough. At a young age, I had a liking for numbers and maths. I was never really good at English and I do not like to write a lot even at this present time. I used my math skills to help me solve problems and went on to do my first degree in mathematics.

Currently I am a high school math teacher and I am doing a full stack bootcamp at SMU. Outside of work , I like going out to the movies, road trips, and out door activities. One of things that keep driving me is that I always think about how to do the work faster and effective at the same time. I know its going to be hard but I have faith in God. With God all things are possible.
